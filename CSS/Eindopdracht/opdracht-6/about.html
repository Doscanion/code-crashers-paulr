ABOUT UMO

Our Story

The company was started in the early 19th century by Uthor Olivar Moret. At that time we had no building and our products were sold on the streets. At that time only necklaces and rings were sold by Moret.

As the company became more well known it had its first shop opened on the Nietbestaanstraat in Vliegweg. With the start of the first shop the first workers were hired for the company. And company grew to what it became now.
